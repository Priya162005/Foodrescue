🎯 UN Sustainable Development Goals alignment

FoodRescue directly contributes to three UN SDGs.

🌾

SDG 2 — Zero Hunger

Ensuring surplus food reaches people facing food insecurity rather than landfills. 4,280+ meals redistributed this month.

♻️

SDG 12 — Responsible Consumption

Cutting food waste at source. By 2030 SDG target is to halve per capita global food waste — we're accelerating that locally.

🌍

SDG 13 — Climate Action

Food waste in landfills produces methane, a potent greenhouse gas. 1,920 kg CO₂e prevented this month alone.
